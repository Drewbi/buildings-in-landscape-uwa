Buildings in Landscape

UWA Historical Society

This is a virtual tour that starts at Winthrop Hall, circumnavigates Whitfeld Court, then voyages on to the Sunken Garden, before finishing in the Tropical Grove.

Look for the information icons to learn more about the history of UWA. Click and drag or swipe on mobile to look around and click the arrow icons to move to the next location.
